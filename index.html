My Trips Around the World

A personal travel journal — places I've been, things I've seen.

🗼

Paris, France

Summer 2023

Walked along the Seine, visited the Louvre, and ate way too many croissants. The Eiffel Tower at night was unforgettable.

🏯

Tokyo, Japan

Spring 2024

Cherry blossoms in Ueno Park, ramen at midnight, and the organized chaos of Shinjuku crossing. A city like no other.

🏜️

Petra, Jordan

Winter 2022

Hiking through the Siq canyon to reach the Treasury was surreal. Ancient history carved straight into the rock.

🌊

Santorini, Greece

Summer 2022

White-washed walls, blue domes, and sunsets over the caldera. The Aegean Sea looked impossibly blue.

Made with love and a lot of miles ✈️
