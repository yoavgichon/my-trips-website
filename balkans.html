Peaks of the Balkans

September 2018

The Journey

The Peaks of the Balkans is a ~192km circular trail passing through the wild mountain border regions of Albania, Montenegro, and Kosovo. Remote villages, dramatic ridgelines, and a trail that crosses three countries — one of the most spectacular multi-day routes in Europe.

Travel Companion

Benjamin Ben.

Highlights

  • Crossing into three countries on foot through the Accursed Mountains (Prokletije)
  • Staying in remote mountain guesthouses along the trail
  • Dramatic high-altitude scenery throughout Albania, Montenegro, and Kosovo
