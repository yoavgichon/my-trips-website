New Zealand

December 27, 2015 – March 23, 2016

The Journey

Nearly three months in New Zealand — my first big adventure after finishing my service in the IDF. One month in the North Island, almost two months in the South Island, moving on foot and living out of a backpack.

Travel Companion

Travelled with my close friend Benjamin Ben.

Highlights

  • North Island: volcanic landscapes, Tongariro Alpine Crossing, hot springs
  • South Island: Milford Sound, the Routeburn and Kepler tracks, glacier country
  • Almost three months of total freedom after years of army service
