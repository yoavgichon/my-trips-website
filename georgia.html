Georgia — Svaneti

August 2017 · ~2 weeks

The Journey

Two weeks in Georgia, centred around a multi-day trek through the remote Svaneti region in the Caucasus mountains. Ancient stone towers, glacier-fed rivers, and villages that feel untouched by time.

Travel Companions

Benjamin Ben, my cousin Eshchar Gichon, and my friend Vladi Shachar.

Highlights

  • Multi-day trek from Mestia to Ushguli — one of the highest continuously inhabited villages in Europe
  • Medieval Svan towers scattered across every valley
  • Several day trips exploring the surrounding mountains
